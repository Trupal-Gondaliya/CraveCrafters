Gujarati

Gujarati cuisine features vegetarian dishes like Dhokla, Thepla, and Undhiyu, blending sweet, savory, and spicy flavors with cultural richness.

Punjabi

Punjabi cuisine highlights hearty vegetarian dishes such as Chole, Saag Paneer, and Aloo Paratha, known for bold flavors and cultural richness.

Bengali

Bengali cuisine offers delicate vegetarian dishes like Shukto, Aloo Posto, and Cholar Dal, featuring subtle spices and cultural heritage flavors.

south Indian

South Indian vegetarian cuisine includes iconic dishes like Dosa, Idli, Sambar, and Vada, known for their diverse flavors and regional culinary traditions.

Maharashtra

Maharashtrian vegetarian cuisine features flavorful dishes like Puran Poli, Misal Pav, and Sabudana Khichdi, highlighting bold flavors and regional specialties.

Rajasthani

Rajasthani vegetarian cuisine is known for dishes like Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi, and Ker Sangri, rich in flavors and cultural heritage.

Kashmiri

Kashmiri vegetarian cuisine features Dum Aloo, Rajma, and Paneer Chaman, celebrated for aromatic spices and rich flavors reflecting regional diversity.

Fast Food

Fast food includes burgers, pizzas, fries, and tacos, known for their convenience and varied flavors, often enjoyed for quick meals.

Cakes

Cakes are sweet desserts made from flour, sugar, and flavorings, often decorated with frosting and enjoyed for celebrations and indulgence.

Cold Drink

Cold drinks, or sodas, are refreshing beverages flavored with cola, lemon-lime, or fruit extracts, enjoyed chilled for their fizzy, thirst-quenching properties.

Salad

A fruit and vegetable salad combines fresh greens with vibrant fruits and vegetables, topped with dressing for a nutritious and flavorful dish.

Ice Cream

Ice cream is a creamy frozen dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings, enjoyed in cones or cups for indulgence.
